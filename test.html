Nice to meet you! I'm Laura: designer, cat lover and amateur cook.

My journey with design began at the age of 12, when I read "The Non-Designer’s Design Book". That early spark led me to pursue a technical course in Multimedia, where I studied design fundamentals alongside Advertising, Photography, and Videomaking.

While earning my bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design, I was introduced to UX Design — and everything changed. I landed an internship at Itaú Unibanco, Brazil’s largest bank and one of the country’s most respected design institutions. During my time there, I realized that UX Design had the power to drive real impact and solve complex business problems. I was hooked.

It’s been over six years since that first encounter with Product Design, and I’m still deeply passionate about the craft. I love bringing ideas to life, collaborating with peers, and shaping concepts into thoughtful, effective solutions.

Outside of work, I enjoy cooking and baking, spending time with my cat Olívia, and reading. I’m also learning to play the guitar and working on my German. If you'd like to chat about design, collaboration, or just grab a coffee and exchange ideas — feel free to reach out. I’m always up for a good conversation.
